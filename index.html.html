Privacy Policy — Tigrigna Learning App

✅ No personal data collected

Brhan Tech ("we", "us", or "our") built the Memhrey – Learn Tigrigna app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Memhrey does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to our servers
  • Any data from children or adults

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Memhrey stores the following information only on your device using local storage (SharedPreferences). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to Brhan Tech or any third party:

  • Your display name (entered voluntarily on first launch)
  • Learning progress per level (completed activities, quiz scores)
  • XP points and streak count
  • Earned certificates

You can clear this data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings.

3. No Internet Connection Required

Memhrey works entirely offline. The app does not make any network requests and does not connect to any servers, APIs, or third-party services.

4. No Third-Party Services

Memhrey does not use any third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or social features. There are no ads in the app.

5. Children's Privacy

Memhrey is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we collect no personal data whatsoever, the app is safe for all ages, but is marketed toward adult learners.

6. Security

Since no personal data is transmitted or stored on external servers, there is no risk of your information being intercepted or breached through our systems.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
